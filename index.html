About Me

The User Experience is my main focus when designing all websites. I want to ensure a seemless, smooth, and natural exploration of websites while remaining responsive on all devices.

I'm self-taught in HTML, CSS, SASS, Javascript, and jQuery. I've also added powerful tools to my arsenal such as Bootstrap 4 and Wordpress. With these technologies, I'll use them to produce interactive and fun experiences for your business!

When I'm not making cool things on my computer, you can usually find me playing some of my favorite games, hanging out for a night of Dungeons & Dragons, or playing with my cat. I'm also a huge foodie and always looking for the next great place to grab a bite!
